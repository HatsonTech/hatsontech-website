About HatsonTech

From Idea to Software, From Software to Innovation.

HatsonTech is an Ankara-based software solutions and innovation company operating in ODTÜ Teknokent. We design and build custom software, web platforms, mobile apps, cloud systems, and AI-powered products. Our flagship product is FlowDesk, an all-in-one business management platform.

Our mission is to build software that solves real problems — from our own products like FlowDesk to tailored solutions for our clients — and to create lasting value for the businesses and people who rely on it. We bring the engineering, cloud, and AI capability behind our own products directly to our clients through our solutions: custom software, web and mobile development, AI & data, and cloud.

Our vision is to become one of Türkiye's leading software and innovation companies, building our own products and platforms that make a difference both locally and internationally.
