Big Data & Data Engineering

Data pipelines, storage, and analytics infrastructure — turning your scattered data into a clean, scalable source AI can use.

What we do

We build data collection, processing pipelines, storage, and analytics infrastructure. We turn your scattered, messy data — spread across different sources — into a reliable, clean, scalable source. Good AI is built on top of good data infrastructure.

Our approach

We do not just store data; we design its flow, quality, and security. With automated processing pipelines, data warehouses, and analytics dashboards, your data becomes ready for both reporting and AI. This infrastructure is also the foundation for the RAG and semantic search solutions you build on top.

Use cases

  • Data collection and automated processing pipelines
  • Data warehousing and reporting infrastructure
  • Data cleaning and quality management
  • Preparing data for AI

Why HatsonTech?

We run our own products on large-scale data; we know the fine points of preparing data for AI. We make data privacy and security part of the design from the start.
