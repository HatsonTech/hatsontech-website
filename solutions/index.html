Solutions & Services

We bring the engineering power that builds our own AI products to your business projects. End-to-end solutions, from software to LLM training, computer vision to big data.

HatsonTech is a software solutions and innovation company based at ODTÜ Teknokent. We build our own products — the all-in-one business management platform caseon.ai, the AI automation and assistant engine that powers it (MevzuatBot), and the e-commerce and retail management platform DiligenceAI. With the same team and the same technology, we also deliver solutions for businesses: we bring the AI, software, and data capability proven in our own products to your project.
